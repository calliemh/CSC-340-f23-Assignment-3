Tabby Cat Coffee is a local coffee shop located in Townville, North Carolina on 111 N. Avenue St.

Our Goal

We aim to provide a cozy space in the community for people and felines alike! Come and visit us Mon-Sat from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

Employee Of The Month

We are thrilled to announce this month's Employee of the Month is none other than Magatha "Maggie" Christie. Maggie has been with us for 9 years and is our second oldest cat here at Tabby Cat Coffee. Maggie has won Employee of the Month for her exceptional customer service skills and for her efforts in responding to constructive criticism (She's finally stopped scratching the bulletin board!). If you would like to congratulate Maggie, she can be found at her favorite scratching post.
